Push-ups

Instructions: Keep your back straight, lower your body until your chest almost touches the ground, then push back up.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Plank

Instructions: Hold a straight body position, supported by your forearms and toes, for as long as possible.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐☆☆

Squats

Instructions: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, lower your body as if sitting back into a chair, and then stand back up.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐☆☆

Lunges

Instructions: Step forward with one leg and lower your hips until both knees are bent at about a 90-degree angle.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Jumping Jacks

Instructions: Jump with your legs spread wide and hands touching above your head, then return to the starting position.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐☆☆☆

Burpees

Instructions: Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat with your hands on the ground, kick back into a plank, then return to squat and jump up.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Bicycle Crunches

Instructions: Lie on your back, lift your legs, and alternate touching your knees with opposite elbows in a pedaling motion.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆

Mountain Climbers

Instructions: Start in a plank position and alternate driving your knees toward your chest as quickly as possible.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Sit-ups

Instructions: Lie on your back with your knees bent, place your hands behind your head, and lift your torso toward your knees.

Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐☆☆

Pull-ups

Instructions: Hang from a pull-up bar with your palms facing away and pull your body up until your chin is above the bar.
